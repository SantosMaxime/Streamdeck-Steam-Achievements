How to use

Press the key to jump straight to the achievement grid profile.

Auto-detect: Automatically picks the right bundled grid profile for your Stream Deck model — Standard, Mini, XL, +, or Neo. No configuration needed.

Choose profile: Enter an exact profile name from your manifest.json (e.g. profiles/grid-standard) for custom configurations.

Place this button on your main profile to always have a one-press shortcut to the achievement grid.
